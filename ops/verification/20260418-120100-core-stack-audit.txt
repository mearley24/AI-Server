5. Better replacements or package suggestions
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Re-validated April 17:
- Docker Compose IS the right shape for single-host ≤20 services
  on macOS. Keep it.
- Nomad: requires a server+agent, adds a control plane for no gain
  on one host. REJECT.
- k3s: too heavy for one Mac Mini. REJECT.
- Compose profiles: MILDLY useful — would cleanly gate x-intake-lab
  (dev-only) out of `docker compose up`. A 2-line change when
  there's appetite for it; not urgent.
- docker-compose.override.yml: not needed until there's a real
  dev/prod split.
- hadolint / dockerfilelint: low-priority nice-to-have; no real
  pain signal yet.

Net verdict: keep compose as-is. Improvements should be config
polish (profiles for dev services, resource limits) not architecture.
