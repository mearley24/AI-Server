## Design notes
- LaunchAgent (not LaunchDaemon): runs as bob, no root needed.
- KeepAlive=true: launchd restarts on crash; ThrottleInterval=30s bounds storms.
- No StartInterval: continuous process, not a tick. Interval controlled by --interval-sec 2.0.
- No UserName key: inherited from LaunchAgent context (bob).
- Handles SIGTERM cleanly (tools/network_dropout_watch.py:20-28).
- Writes state to data/network_watch/dropout_watch_status.json (read by watchdog / Cortex).
- Outbound surface: ICMP ping to 192.168.1.1 (gateway), 1.1.1.1 (WAN). No inbound ports.
