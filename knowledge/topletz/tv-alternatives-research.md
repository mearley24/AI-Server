## High-End Residential Smart Home Project
*Research compiled: March 30, 2026 | For CI/Integrator Use*

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The client's current TV selections are all consumer-grade products sold through retail channels (Best Buy, Amazon, Costco). For a high-end residential project with Control4 integration, there are meaningful pro-grade alternatives available through Snap One, WAVE Electronics, and other CI distributors. The key advantages of pro-grade selections include: better CI channel support (dealer-only pricing and warranty), native Control4 IP drivers, flush-wall mounting options, longer panel warranties, and superior image quality at large sizes.

| Spec | Value |

| Lifespan | 100,000+ hours |

**AWALL Brand Background:**
AWALL is a US-based (Florida) MicroLED display company that won the **Best of Show Award at InfoComm 2025** and an award at **CEDIA 2025**. They position themselves as the most affordable CoB MicroLED on the market — pricing from $8,250 for the 75" up to $56,900 for larger sizes, compared to competing MicroLED walls priced $70,000–$1.2M. The brand operates a dealer portal at awall.com/pages/dealer and is sold through custom integrators.

**Distribution:** AWALL sells direct via awall.com and through CI/AV dealers including Dreamedia AV. They appeared at CEDIA 2025, signaling growing CI channel traction. Available on Best Buy for some sizes. WAVE Electronics does not appear to carry AWALL currently. Snap One does not carry AWALL. Direct dealer program available at awall.com/pages/dealer.

**Key Advantages for Art Wall / Large Format:**
- True MicroLED technology — modular, no burn-in risk (unlike OLED)
- Nearly flat/flush to wall — 3mm panel depth
- 1,200 nits brightness — better in bright rooms than OLED
- 16:9 aspect ratio in 108" or 21:9 in 102" for cinematic format
- IP65-rated — extremely durable
- Modular — can be sized to exact room requirements (custom aspect ratios available)
- 100,000+ hour lifespan — significantly longer than OLED (~30,000–50,000 hrs)

**Key Concerns:**
- The 108" model weighs ~220 lb — heavier than alternatives; ceiling mount would need substantial engineering
- Assembly required for sizes above 81" (two-person job, ~2 hours; professional install ~$4,000–$6,000 additional)
- Not native 4K at all sizes (108" 16:9 is 1920x1080 or 2560x1440 at 1.2mm pitch; 162" achieves 4K at 0.9mm pitch)
- No native Control4 driver known — would require IP/RS-232 or IR control workaround
- Viewing distance matters — 1.2mm pitch requires minimum ~8ft viewing distance to avoid visible pixel structure
- New brand — commercial track record is limited compared to Samsung/LG/Sony

**Recommendation for Hearth Room Ceiling Mount:** AWALL is a compelling conversation piece for an art wall application but the ceiling mount weight (~220 lb for 108") and assembly complexity make it better suited for a wall installation. For Hearth Room ceiling mount, Samsung QN90F 98" or Sony 98" X90L are safer structural choices.
