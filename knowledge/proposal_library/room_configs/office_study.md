## TYPICAL SYSTEMS IN THIS ROOM

- Lighting control (good quality dimming for video call lighting and focus work)
- Motorized shades (glare control for computer screens and video calls)
- Audio (in-ceiling background music for focus; privacy for conference calls)
- AV (secondary display or media in study)
- Climate (zone sensor if private room)
- Door lock (privacy lock or smart lock if client works from home with family)

## COMMON CLIENT REQUESTS — OFFICE / STUDY

- **"I need good lighting for Zoom calls"** → "Video Call" preset: front-facing overhead at 70%, task light at 50%, shades at 40% open for soft natural backlight. This is a specific and popular request.
- **"Can I lock the office door so the kids don't interrupt?"** → Smart lock on office door (Schlage or Yale) integrated with Control4. "Do Not Disturb" mode: lock the door via keypad, send visual indicator to family panels ("Dad's on a call").
- **"I want to listen to music but not disturb the rest of the house"** → Office is an independent audio zone. Only office speakers play; whole-home system unaffected.
- **"Can I see who's at the door without leaving my desk?"** → Doorbell camera pop-up on office TV or secondary monitor via Control4 driver.
