always_include: true

Following project completion, any additional service, modifications, or support not covered under warranty or an active service plan will be billable.

This includes system adjustments, reprogramming, device additions, troubleshooting, and client-requested changes after commissioning.

Service rates are defined in the project agreement (Section 5).

Remote support is billed in 15-minute increments. Minor adjustments within the sixty (60) day tuning period are included at no additional charge.
