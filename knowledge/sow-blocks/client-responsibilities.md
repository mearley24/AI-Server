The client and project team are responsible for providing a construction environment ready for installation, including proper pathways, backing, power, and site readiness.

All high-voltage infrastructure, including circuits and terminations, shall be provided by a licensed electrician.

The client is responsible for maintaining active internet service and any required third-party accounts necessary for system operation.

Timely approvals and decisions are required to maintain project schedule.

Wireless performance may vary due to building materials, environmental conditions, and device usage.

Installed equipment must be protected from damage by other trades or construction activity.

Modifications or service performed by others after commissioning may impact system performance.
