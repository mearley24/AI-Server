Build and commission a centralized equipment rack within the designated space, allowing proper service access and long-term maintainability.

Install managed power distribution with remote reboot capability for system stability and service efficiency.

Install UPS backup for control and network equipment only. High-draw devices such as amplifiers are excluded from UPS support.

A dedicated 20A circuit will be installed by the project electrician at a location approved collaboratively by the client, Symphony Smart Homes, and the electrician.

All high-voltage work is performed by others. Symphony Smart Homes is not responsible for electrical infrastructure, circuit performance, or power quality.
