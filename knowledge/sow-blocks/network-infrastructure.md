Install and configure network infrastructure including gateway, switches, and wireless access points to support system-wide performance.

Implement VLAN segmentation for Management, Control, AV, Security, and Guest networks to ensure stability and organization across all connected systems. The Qolsys IQ Panel 4 and secondary keypad will be segmented on a dedicated Security VLAN (VLAN 40).

Provide a structured network backbone supporting control, audio streaming, security panel communication, and device communication throughout the residence.

Network performance will be validated at time of commissioning. Ongoing performance may be influenced by internet service provider conditions, environmental factors, and connected device usage.
