Install a centralized rack-based amplification system supporting audio zones throughout the home's common areas and bedrooms as defined in the approved plan.

Integrate Triad amplification, TS-PAMP8-125-V2 and TS-PAMP4-125-V2 or approved equivalent, to deliver consistent performance across all zones.

Install and commission in-ceiling and outdoor speakers based on final layout and field conditions.

Configure zone control, source access, grouping behavior, and playback stability through Control4.

Speaker placement is designed to provide balanced coverage and an optimal listening experience in each space. Minor adjustments may be required based on structure or site conditions.
