Review approved plans, RFP requirements, and room-by-room intent specific to the {{project_name}} prior to installation.

Coordinate with the general contractor ({{gc}}), project electrician, and design team on rough-in pathways, centralized rack location, iPad mounting locations and in-wall power, wireless access point placement, security panel placement, and all device mounting locations.

Confirm all required backing, conduit, and pathways are in place prior to rough-in to support a clean and efficient installation aligned with the project schedule.

Final device locations, quantities, and system configuration are based on approved plans and site conditions at time of installation.

Field adjustments may be required to accommodate structural elements, code requirements, or coordination with other trades.

Produce field-ready rough and trim documentation along with a final integration map for commissioning and long-term service reference.
