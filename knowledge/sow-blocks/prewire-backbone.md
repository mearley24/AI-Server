Install and terminate low-voltage cabling supporting control, audio, network, shade, camera, and security panel systems throughout the residence.

Install RG6/U coaxial cabling at designated locations for future video service needs and long-term flexibility.

Install structured network cabling, Cat6 or better, to all required device locations to support both current system functionality and future expansion. Primary TV locations will receive 2x Cat6 (one for streaming device, one for control/future use).

All cabling will be home-run to centralized rack or head-end locations for system reliability and serviceability.

### General Prewire Terms

Cabling installation assumes unobstructed pathways at time of rough-in. Additional labor resulting from blocked pathways, finished surfaces, or rework conditions will be addressed via change order.

All wiring will be labeled, tested, and documented to support long-term reliability and ease of service.
