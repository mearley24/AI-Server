# Triad TS-PDX-IC64 PDX Series In-Ceiling Speaker 6"

## Overview
A 6-inch 2-way in-ceiling speaker from Triad's PDX performance series. Designed for distributed audio in residential applications with a focus on balanced, room-filling sound from a flush-mount form factor.

## Specs
- Drivers: 6.5" woofer, 1" silk dome tweeter
- Frequency Response: 55Hz–20kHz
- Impedance: 8 ohms
- Sensitivity: 87dB
- Power Handling: 10–125W
- Cutout: ~8.5" round
- Mounting: Spring-loaded dog-ear clips

## Symphony Usage
Standard in-ceiling speaker for all interior audio zones. Typically installed in pairs (stereo) per zone. Paired with Episode speaker brackets (ES-ESS-BRKT-IC-6) for new construction pre-install before drywall. Connected to Triad amplification system.
