# Control4 Contemporary 120V Adaptive Phase Dimmer

## Overview
A standard single-load dimmer that automatically detects whether to use forward or reverse phase dimming based on the connected load. Part of Control4's native lighting platform, communicating over ZigBee.

## Specs
- Load Rating: 120V, up to 600W incandescent / 250W LED (adaptive phase)
- Dimming: Auto-detect forward/reverse phase
- Communication: ZigBee mesh
- Style: Contemporary series
- Color: Snow White (SW)
- Requires: Neutral wire

## Symphony Usage
The workhorse dimmer used on most lighting circuits. Placed wherever dimming is needed but dedicated scene-control buttons are not required. Paired with keypad dimmers at entry points for full room control.
