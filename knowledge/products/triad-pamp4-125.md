# Triad TS-PAMP4-125-V2 4-Zone Power Amplifier

## Overview
A 4-zone, 125W per channel power amplifier designed for distributed audio. Functions as a standalone unit for smaller projects or an expansion amplifier alongside the PAMP8.

## Specs
- Channels: 4 (stereo pairs = 2 stereo zones or 4 mono zones)
- Power: 125W per channel at 8 ohms
- Inputs: 4 stereo analog inputs
- Control: RS-232, IP, Trigger
- Rack Space: 1U
- Cooling: Fan-cooled

## Symphony Usage
Used alongside the PAMP8 when the project requires more than 8 audio zones. Also used as the sole amplifier on smaller projects or guest houses. Connected through the AMS16 audio matrix switch.
