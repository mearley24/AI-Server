# Episode ES-ESS-BRKT-IC-6 In-Ceiling Speaker Bracket

## Overview
A pre-construction speaker bracket that mounts between ceiling joists before drywall is installed. Holds the speaker cutout position and provides a solid mounting platform for the Triad in-ceiling speakers.

## Specs
- Compatibility: 6" in-ceiling speakers (Triad PDX-IC64 and similar)
- Material: Galvanized steel
- Mounting: Attaches to ceiling joists, adjustable width
- Install Timing: Before drywall (rough-in phase)

## Symphony Usage
Installed during the prewire/rough-in phase on every in-ceiling speaker location. One bracket per speaker. Ensures precise speaker placement and provides a solid mount that prevents rattling. Critical for new construction — allows drywallers to cut around the bracket cleanly.
