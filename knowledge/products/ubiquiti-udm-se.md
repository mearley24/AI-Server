# Ubiquiti UDM-SE Dream Machine Special Edition

## Overview
An all-in-one network appliance combining router, firewall, 8-port managed switch (with PoE), and UniFi Network/Protect applications. Serves as the network gateway and management platform for the entire home network.

## Specs
- WAN: 1x 2.5GbE, 1x 1GbE (failover)
- LAN: 8x 1GbE (4x PoE+, total 60W PoE budget)
- SFP+: 1x 10G SFP+ (expansion)
- Storage: 128GB internal SSD for UniFi Protect (NVR)
- Applications: UniFi Network, UniFi Protect, UniFi Access, UniFi Talk
- VLAN: Full VLAN support with inter-VLAN routing
- Form Factor: Desktop/rack mount (1U with adapter)

## Symphony Usage
Standard network gateway for all residential projects. Manages VLAN segmentation (Management, Control, AV, Security, Guest), firewall rules, and threat management. The built-in NVR supports camera recording if/when cameras are added. Rack-mounted in the equipment rack.
