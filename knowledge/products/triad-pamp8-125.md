# Triad TS-PAMP8-125-V2 8-Zone Power Amplifier

## Overview
An 8-zone, 125W per channel power amplifier designed for distributed audio systems. Each zone is independently controllable via Control4, supporting different sources and volume levels per zone.

## Specs
- Channels: 8 (stereo pairs = 4 stereo zones or 8 mono zones)
- Power: 125W per channel at 8 ohms
- Inputs: 8 stereo analog inputs
- Control: RS-232, IP, Trigger
- Rack Space: 2U
- Cooling: Fan-cooled, front-to-rear airflow

## Symphony Usage
Primary amplifier in the equipment rack for distributed audio. Paired with the TS-PAMP4-125-V2 when more than 8 zones are needed. Connected to the Triad AMS16 audio matrix switch for flexible source routing to any zone.
