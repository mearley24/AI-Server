# Strong SR-WMS-18U Wall Mount Rack System

## Overview
An 18U wall-mount equipment rack designed for residential AV and network installations. Provides organized, ventilated housing for all rack-mount equipment with swing-out access for service.

## Specs
- Rack Units: 18U
- Depth: Adjustable (typically 18"–22")
- Mount: Wall mount with swing-out frame
- Material: Welded steel, powder coated
- Weight Capacity: Up to 150 lbs
- Ventilation: Perforated top and sides
- Accessories: Compatible with Strong shelves, panels, and power strips

## Symphony Usage
Standard equipment rack for residential projects. Houses the CORE3 controller, network switches, gateway, amplifiers, audio matrix, power distribution, and UPS. Wall-mounted in a dedicated closet or utility space. Location is coordinated with the GC and electrician to ensure proper ventilation, power access, and service clearance.
