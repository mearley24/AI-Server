# Control4 Contemporary 120/277V Switch

## Overview
A connected on/off switch for non-dimmable loads such as bathroom exhaust fans, closet lights on non-dimmable fixtures, and utility circuits. Part of Control4's native lighting platform.

## Specs
- Load Rating: 120V or 277V, up to 15A
- Dimming: None (on/off only)
- Communication: ZigBee mesh
- Style: Contemporary series
- Color: Snow White (SW)
- Requires: Neutral wire

## Symphony Usage
Used for any circuit that should not be dimmed — exhaust fans, garbage disposals, non-dimmable fixtures, and utility loads. Provides automation control (scheduling, scenes) without dimming capability.
