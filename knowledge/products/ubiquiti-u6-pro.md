# Ubiquiti U6-PRO WiFi 6 Access Point

## Overview
A WiFi 6 (802.11ax) ceiling-mount access point providing high-performance wireless coverage. PoE-powered for clean, single-cable installation. Managed through the UniFi Network application on the UDM-SE.

## Specs
- WiFi: 802.11ax (WiFi 6), dual-band 2.4GHz + 5GHz
- Max Throughput: 5.3 Gbps aggregate
- Clients: 300+ concurrent
- Power: 802.3at PoE (48V)
- Mounting: Ceiling mount (flush), wall mount adapter available
- VLAN: Multiple SSIDs per VLAN
- Coverage: ~140m² (1,500 sq ft) typical per AP

## Symphony Usage
Installed at ceiling-mount locations per the wireless coverage plan. Quantity determined by home size, construction materials, and coverage requirements. Each AP broadcasts multiple SSIDs mapped to VLANs (Management, Control, Guest, etc.). PoE-powered from the USW-PRO-24-POE switch.
