# Binary Audio Cables (Assorted)

## Overview
Standard audio interconnect cables used for rack-level connections between audio sources, matrix switches, and amplifiers. Binary is the Snap One house brand for cables and connectivity.

## Models Used
- **B5-AUD-1** — Premium analog audio cable, 1m (RCA stereo pair)
- **B5-AUD-2** — Premium analog audio cable, 2m (RCA stereo pair)
- **B5-DIGCOAX-1** — Digital coaxial audio cable, 1m
- **B3-STEREOMINIRCA-1** — 3.5mm stereo mini to RCA adapter cable, 1m

## Symphony Usage
Ordered per project based on the rack layout and source/amplifier configuration. B5-AUD cables connect sources to the AMS16 matrix switch and matrix outputs to amplifier inputs. Digital coax is used for digital audio connections (CA-1 to matrix or direct to amp). The mini-to-RCA adapter is used for devices with 3.5mm output only.
