Vail Creek Manor — Dedicated Theater

Vail, CO Completed December 2025 1,100 sq ft

Project Overview

14-seat Dolby Atmos theater with JVC NZ9 laser projector, 140" Stewart Filmscreen, Trinnov Altitude32 processor, Seating by Fortress.

Carved into the lower level of a ski-in ski-out estate, this dedicated theater was designed from the concrete up with acoustics in mind. Bass traps and diffusion panels were installed before framing. A JVC NZ9 8K laser projector throws onto a 140-inch Stewart Filmscreen Phantom HALR for reference-quality HDR. Nineteen drivers — a combination of Focal and Seaton Sound subs — are managed by a Trinnov Altitude32 for Dolby Atmos object-based audio. Control4 integration provides a single-button experience: seats recline, lights fade to amber, and the popcorn maker starts.

"I've seen commercial cinemas with worse picture and sound. Symphony built something genuinely special down here."

D. Harrington Homeowner, Vail
