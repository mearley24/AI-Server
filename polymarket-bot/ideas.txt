IDEA: EnsembleForecastEdge — use multiple weather models instead of just NOAA GFS
DATE: 2026-04-02
DESCRIPTION: Combine NOAA GFS, ECMWF (European model), and NAM (North American Mesoscale) for a consensus forecast. When all three agree, buy the bracket aggressively.
HYPOTHESIS: Ensemble agreement reduces sigma from 3.5°F to ~1.5°F. This dramatically reprices adjacent brackets. A 10°F bracket that should be 8¢ based on GFS alone should be 3¢ if all models agree it's 12°F off. Market rarely prices in ensemble consensus.
