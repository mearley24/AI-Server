## Section 6 — Terms & Conditions

### 6.1 Term & Renewal
This agreement is for a **12-month term** beginning on [START_DATE]. 
It will automatically renew for successive 12-month terms unless either party 
provides written notice of non-renewal at least **30 days** prior to the 
renewal date.

### 6.2 Cancellation
Either party may cancel this agreement with **30 days written notice**. 
If client cancels within the first 6 months, a cancellation fee equal to 
**2 months of the monthly equivalent** of the agreement price will apply.

### 6.3 Service Scheduling
Preventive maintenance visits will be scheduled at a mutually agreeable time. 
Client is responsible for providing site access at the scheduled time. 
Rescheduling with less than 24-hour notice may result in a rescheduling fee.

### 6.4 Limitation of Liability
Symphony Smart Homes' liability under this agreement is limited to the 
annual value of the agreement. Symphony is not liable for consequential, 
incidental, or indirect damages.
