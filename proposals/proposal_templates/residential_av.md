## Section 6 — Terms & Conditions

### 6.1 Warranty
- All Symphony installations carry a **1-year labor warranty** covering any 
  installation defects or workmanship issues.
- Equipment warranties are passed through from the manufacturer per their 
  published terms.
- Extended service agreements are available — see Symphony's Maintenance 
  Agreement for details.

### 6.2 Change Orders
- Any additions or modifications to the scope of work after contract execution 
  require a written Change Order signed by both parties.
- Change Orders will include updated pricing and schedule impact.

### 6.3 Client Responsibilities
- Client shall ensure site access during scheduled installation windows.
- Client is responsible for ensuring adequate electrical circuits are in place 
  per Symphony's pre-installation checklist.
- Client is responsible for final paint and drywall repair if wall penetrations 
  are required.

### 6.4 Exclusions
This proposal does not include:
- Electrical rough-in or panel upgrades
- Structural modifications
- Network infrastructure (unless explicitly included in scope)
- Furniture or cabinetry

### 6.5 Proposal Validity
This proposal is valid for **30 days** from the date of issue. 
Pricing is subject to change after the validity period.
